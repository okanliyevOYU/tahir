My Table Tennis Journey

Passion for Ping Pong

I discovered table tennis when I was 7 years old at a local community center. I instantly fell in love with the fast-paced action and the sound of the ball hitting the table.

My parents noticed my talent and got me a coach when I was 8. Since then, I've been training seriously and competing in tournaments for my age group.

My goal is to become a national champion in my age category and eventually play in international tournaments. My favorite players are Ma Long and Tomokazu Harimoto.
